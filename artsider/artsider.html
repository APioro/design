Artsider Foundation

The Artsider Foundation, based in Kraków, is dedicated to increasing the visibility of artists living with schizophrenia. Its mission is to replace stigma with dialogue, showing that art created in the context of mental health challenges has the same power to move, inspire, and connect people. The foundation needed a visual identity that reflects these values: inclusivity, optimism, and authenticity — something that speaks both to the warmth of the community and the individual vision of each artist. While their works often appear on mugs in cafés, Artsider’s impact goes much further: it is about creating a cultural space where creativity is seen as universal and diversity becomes a source of beauty and strength.
