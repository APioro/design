Astrolume

Brand concept for an amateur astronomy community, designed to combine curiosity with knowledge through visually engaging materials, accessible information, and a shared passion for the cosmos. Astrolume stands out by valuing both the awe and beauty of stargazing and scientific accuracy. The brand’s mission is to inspire people to look up at the sky rather than at their phone screens.

The visual language balances restraint and emotion: bold, expressive graphics are complemented by subtle scientific accents that lend credibility. This approach avoids associations with astrology while evoking the sense of wonder and admiration that comes with observing the night sky. The result is a system that is both poetic and reliable—inviting discovery and curiosity while maintaining scientific integrity.
