Bowl'd

Bowl’d is a conceptual brand exploring how food design can be personal, urban, and slightly rebellious. Created as an independent project, it redefines the typical salad bar experience — replacing cheerful greens and soft pastels with a darker, more experimental tone. It targets urban creatives and professionals who want food that is personal, not performative. Bowl’d rejects the artificial freshness typical of salad brands. It is direct, slightly raw, and grounded in how people actually eat — differently every day, mixing what is good with what tastes good.

The brand identity combines raw, tangible elements with modern minimalism. A hand-cut logo, intentionally imperfect, reflects the DIY spirit and personalization. Ink-printed vegetables merge with real photography, creating a collage system that feels crafted and alive.



A monochromatic palette sets the tone — urban, unpolished, and authentic — allowing the vibrant colors of ingredients to be the true heroes. Together, the visuals create a brand language that is confident, grounded, and clearly distinct from the polished, cheerful look typical of health food brands.
